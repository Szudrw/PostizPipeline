Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the application referred to here as the "Application" handles information. The Application is a private, self-hosted content-scheduling tool operated by Arross (the "Operator") for the Operator's own personal use. It is used to schedule and publish the Operator's own short-form video content to the Operator's own connected social media accounts, and to retrieve performance statistics for those posts.

The Application is not offered to the general public as a product, is not listed in any app store, and is not intended to be used by anyone other than the Operator and accounts the Operator personally authorizes.

1. Information the Application Accesses

The Application only accesses information for social media accounts that have been explicitly authorized by their owner through each platform's official login (OAuth) consent screen. Depending on the connected platform, this may include:

  • Basic account information — such as the account's unique identifier, username, and display name, used to identify which authorized account to publish to.
  • Authorization tokens — the access and refresh tokens issued by the platform when an account is connected, used solely to act on that account on the Operator's behalf.
  • Content provided by the Operator — the video files, captions, titles, descriptions, and scheduling details the Operator submits for publishing.
  • Post performance metrics — such as views, likes, comments, and shares on content published through the Application, retrieved for the Operator's own review.

The Application does not collect data from people who view or interact with the published content, and does not access private messages, contacts, or any data beyond what is needed to publish content and read its performance.

2. How Information Is Used

The information above is used only to:

  • Publish the Operator's own video content to the Operator's own authorized accounts;
  • Schedule that content to be published at chosen times;
  • Retrieve and display performance statistics for that content to the Operator.

The Application does not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the functions above.

3. How Information Is Stored

All data is stored on infrastructure controlled by the Operator (a self-hosted server environment). Authorization tokens are stored securely and are used only to communicate with the official APIs of the connected platforms. The Operator does not operate shared servers on behalf of other users.

4. Information Sharing

The Application does not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties. The only external parties that receive data are the social media platforms themselves (for example TikTok, Meta/Instagram, and Google/YouTube), and only through their official APIs, for the sole purpose of publishing content to, or reading statistics from, the account that authorized the connection. Each platform's own handling of that data is governed by its respective privacy policy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Authorization for a connected account can be revoked at any time from within the connected platform's own settings (for example, the "Apps and Websites" or "Connected Apps" section of the account), which immediately ends the Application's ability to access that account. To request deletion of any stored data associated with the Application, contact the Operator at the address below, and the relevant tokens and stored data will be removed.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in the relevant jurisdiction).

7. Compliance With Platform Terms

The Application is used in accordance with the developer terms and content guidelines of each connected platform, including the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and Meta Platform Terms. Use of each platform's API is limited to the permissions (scopes) the account owner has granted.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
